Welcome to Connect Four! In this game, each player alternates placing a piece onto a column until either all columns are full, or one player gets four of their own pieces in a row!

You can play by clicking on the column you want to place your piece on, or using the number keys (1-7) to place a piece in its corresponding column.

You can reset the game by pressing 'r' or the reset button.

Source code for this game can be found here. Press 'e' or the x-button to close.
